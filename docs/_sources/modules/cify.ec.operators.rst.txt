Evolutionary Operators
==========================

There are three types of evolutionary operators for the three stages of simulated evolution; crossover,
mutation and selection. These operators take a variety of parameters that can be passed as a ``dict``
to your chosen evolutionary algorithm. For the differential evolution algorithm, take a look at its
page for a list of trial vector and target vector operators.
